Childhood, Age, Height, Family

Born as Catherine Elise Blanchett on May 14, 1969, in Melbourne, Victoria, Australia, to June (née Gamble) and Robert DeWitt Blanchett, Jr., Cate was raised in an upper-middle-class family.

Besides, Blanchett also has a sister younger than her, Genevieve Blanchett, who was born in 1971. Likewise, her older brother Bob Blanchett was born in 1968.

Marriage With Husband Andrew Upton & Children

Do they have children together?

Cate and Andrew are parents to 4 children altogether, three sons and an adopted daughter. Their first son Dashiell John Upton was born in 2001, their second child Roman Robert Upton was born in 2004, and their third son Ignatius Martin Upton was born in 2008.
