Alex Rodriguez Is Dating Jaclyn Cordeiro Following His Split From Kathryne Padgett

 Posted on April 9, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  461 words  |  Larita Shotwell

On Monday, October 17, 2022, Alex Rodriguez was photographed with fitness instructor Jaclyn Cordeiro on Rodeo Drive in Beverly Hills, California. The couple strolled down the street hand in hand, stopping at a stylish store. According to a Page Six source, Rodriguez is content and enjoying his time with Cordeiro. The revelation comes two months after Alex’s divorce from Kathryne “Kat” Padgett. According to a source, despite their breakup, they will stay excellent friends.

After they started dating in 2017, the former professional baseball shortstop earned attention for his connection with Jennifer Lopez. They announced their engagement in March 2019 and divorced the following year. Lopez later married Ben Affleck, and Alex wished her well in the future, adding that the relationship was a positive experience for him. Rodriguez is now residing in Los Angeles, but will shortly return to work as an MLB Championship Series announcer.

From businesswoman to mother of two daughters: Here’s all you need to know about Jaclyn Cordeiro.

Jaclyn Cordeiro is a fitness influencer and the creator of a fitness program named JaclynFit, according to her Instagram page. She identifies as a Lifestyle Transformation Specialist/RN. She is a native of Ontario, where she attended St. Thomas of Villanova Catholic Secondary School and subsequently the University of Windsor, according to her Facebook profile. Her siblings include two boys, and she enjoys sports such as soccer, taekwondo, and boxing.

Jaclyn Cordeiro has a bachelor’s degree in science as well as honors in nursing. While speaking with Oxygen, she revealed that she discovered weightlifting and fell in love with it. This helped her go through nursing school and stay focused on her life objectives.

She has two kids, aged eight and nine. In an interview with Status last year, she said that her life ambition is to have kids who are strong, independent, and caring. She participated in the Canadian Physique Alliance competition after the birth of her first child, and three more times in the natural class as a bikini fitness CPA plant-based athlete. In a conversation with Status, she said,

“I understood what it needed to get into competition condition, so I competed 5 months to the day after having my first child and came in second place in my local OPA. Then, only two months after competing, I discovered I was pregnant with my second kid. I chose to participate again 5 months after delivering my second child. I finished fourth in the Bikini category.”

A plant-based diet is beneficial to one’s health, according to Jaclyn Cordeiro. She said that her diet assisted her in maintaining physical health as well as mental clarity and function. She noted that she can now maintain a slimmer physique and minimize GI troubles, and she feels a strong connection to her body.
