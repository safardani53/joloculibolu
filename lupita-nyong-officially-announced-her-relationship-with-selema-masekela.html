Lupita Nyong Officially Announced Her Relationship With Selema Masekela

 Posted on February 7, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  442 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Lupita Nyong’o surprised her admirers on Friday by formally revealing her romance with famous broadcaster and pundit Selema Masekela. Her most recent Instagram video showed the pair dressed in various costumes, including robes, swimming suits, and formal. Lupita’s video included the song The King’s Affirmation. The caption was straightforward:

“We just click!” #thisismylove #nuffsaid @selema

The couple, who were seen together at Selema Masekela’s firm launch in Venice, California, did not share any information about their connection.

Selema Masekela is a sports analyst and TV personality.

Selema Masekela was born on August 28, 1971, in Staten Island, New York. Masekela has worked with Transworld Publications, TW Snow, TW Skateboarding, and TW Surf in the past. He has presented various programs and achieved prominence as an ESPN journalist at the 2010 FIFA World Cup. Selema has hosted a variety of live events, and his music has appeared in films such as Entourage and House of Lies. He was also a major Olympic Zone reporter for NBC. Selema has appeared in Point Break, Uncle Drew, and Sneakerheads.

Selema Masekela has been involved in community service and has founded groups such as Stoked Mentoring, The Lunchbox Fund, and The Skatepark Project. Stoked Mentoring has been striving to reduce the risk of deadly accidents in a variety of sports. The Lunchbox Fund provides lunches to pupils in township schools, while The Skatepark Project creates the greatest skating parks.

What has Lupita Nyong’o lately been up to?

Lupita Nyong’o has become a social media sensation after confirming her romance with Selema Masekela. She was also recently seen dancing to Jose Feliciano’s Feliz Navidad with Black Panther: Wakanda Forever co-star Tenoch Huerta on December 22.

Nyong’o shared a video on Instagram of herself and Huerta showing off their skills near the pool. Lupita was dressed in gold, while Tenoch was dressed in an orange suit with silver shoes. Nyong’o captured her time working on the set of the Black Panther sequel with a few pictures she uploaded on Instagram two weeks ago. She had already tweeted some behind-the-scenes photos from the sequel’s set.

Michaela Coel, Winston Duke, Florence Kasumba, and Dania Gurira appeared in the photos. The 39-year-old has not revealed any information about her next ventures. Her most recent MCU cameo was in the newly released Black Panther: Wakanda Forever. It garnered $790 million at the box office as a sequel to the 2018 blockbuster Black Panther.

Black Panther: Wakanda Forever garnered positive reviews from reviewers and moviegoers, mostly for paying homage to Chadwick Boseman, who debuted as Black Panther in Captain America: Civil War in 2016. Boseman died on August 28, 2020, as a result of complications from colon cancer.
