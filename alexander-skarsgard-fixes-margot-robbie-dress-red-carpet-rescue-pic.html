Margot was especially lucky to have her co-star by her side at the The Legend of Tarzan‘s Europeans premiere in London, where she suffered a bit of a wardrobe mishap. Thanks to Alexander, who aided in the repair of her dress, she played it off pretty darn cool.

The True Blood star was there to save her by stepping behind and fixing the back of her formfitting dress. Of course, the Calvin Klein stunner and Vogue covergirl looked flawless even with her unexpected situation. Aside from the malfunction, Margot’s gown totally stole the show. Her nude and black vertical striped ensemble flowed in the wind, and had incredible white detail.

Margot looked adorable as she appeared to be a bit embarrassed, but it happens, girl! The Legend Of Tarzan hit theaters July 1, and he and Margot share a steamy kiss in the film. And Alexander is no stranger to great kisses either. The actor once bragged about his on-screen kiss with Ryan Kwanten, 39, during a steamy sex scene in Season 7 of True Blood. Sadly, Margot didn’t rave about her time with Leonardo DiCaprio, 41, as she said their sex scene was “so awkward!”
