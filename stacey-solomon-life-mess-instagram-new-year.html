Stacey Solomon says her life a 'mess' as she makes New Year's resolution - and fans can get involved

 Posted on May 25, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  540 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

STACEY Solomon has planned a New Year's resolution after admitting to her fans that her life is a "mess".

The pregnant star - who revealed she was expecting her fifth baby earlier this week - asked her fans to help her get stuck in as he headed into "nesting" mode.

Stacey, 33, who is married to Joe Swash, took to her Instagram last night to reveal her hopes and plans for 2023.

The TV star - who recently featured in her own programme titled Sort Your Life Out - is well-known for her 'Tap to Tidy' posts on her social media.

But, despite appearances, the mother-of-four said she felt she had neglected "tidying" up her life in 2022.

Stacey told her Instagram followers she planned to go into "tidy turbo mode" as she needed "a good clear out".

The former X Factor star, who renovated her home and got married last year, admitted she was "on a mission" with her New Year's resolution for 2023.

"Nesting and New Year, tiny turbo mode," she wrote to her followers just before midnight struck.

"I honestly can't sit still. I feel like I spent 2022 sorting lovely people's lives out, and mine is a bit of a mess.

"So I'm going to try and get myself sorted this year, I feel like I'm going to be that annoying person on a mission before baby comes.

"I'm going to try and sort myself out one small thing at a time in Jan, so if anyone fancies a good clear out and start over, maybe we can do it together and motivate each other.

"I am so excited to start nesting, Jan 2023 let's do this."

Stacey went on to wish all her followers a "Happy New Year" at midnight, as she and Joe, 40, lay in bed together.

"From us two old foggies, wishing you all a happy, healthy 20223 filled with strength and love," she added.

It comes after Stacey showed off her huge baby bump last night as she vowed to fill fans in on her life.

She cradled her tummy in a new snap after shocking followers by revealing she is already eight months pregnant.

Captioning the shot, Stacey wrote: “Happy New Year’s Eve everyone. I feel like we have so much to catch up on in 2023.”

She then invited her followers to ask her questions, which she says she will answer at some point today.

No doubt her fans will be keen to learn more about her pregnancy, which Stacey only revealed over Christmas.
