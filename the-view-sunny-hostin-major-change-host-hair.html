The View's Sunny Hostin shocks fans as they spot major change to host's appearance

 Posted on January 1, 0001  |  3 minutes  |  491 words  |  Martina Birk

THE View's Sunny Hostin has debuted a brand new look as she and her fellow co-hosts take some time off from the program.

Fans have quickly spotted a major change to the TV personality’s appearance, and they think she looks “incredible.”

While Sunny’s latest change wasn’t as intense as the plastic surgery she recently underwent, her followers still couldn’t help but gush about it.

On Sunday, the View host, who is currently on break from the show amid its short hiatus, shared photos from an event she recently attended and shared how much she enjoyed it.

Sunny wrote: “What an experience last night to see Ariana [DeBose]’s one woman show at Lincoln Center. 

“The voice, the range, the tone , the dancing, the humor. The EXCELLENCE. What a gift.”

Sunny isn’t the only View host to make changes to her appearance lately.

Last month, Whoopi Goldberg unveiled a brand new look during a live broadcast of the talk show after undergoing an operation.

Fans immediately noticed the 67-year-old's changed appearance as she was not wearing her usual glasses while moderating the show.

Whoopi's co-host Sunny was the first to point out the change-up, prompting the actress to discuss her recent procedure.

"I had an operation, and they replaced the lens," she began, pointing to her left eye.

"And the lens they replaced it with is kind of like my eyeglass lens."

Sunny excitingly commented, "You can see!" to which the audience applauded.

Whoopi then referenced a previous episode where she had trouble reading the teleprompter without her glasses, forcing her to put them on.

Her co-hosts were very interested in the procedure and chimed in with questions for the host.

Fellow panelist Ana Navarro, 51, asked Whoopi to clarify that her surgery differed from cataract surgery, to which the TV star agreed.

She further explained that the lens was replaced using "teeny tiny cataracts," enabling her to regain her eyesight.

Viewers were big fans of Whoopi's new look, based on the flood of comments on social media.

"I knew #WhoopiGoldberg look different. Okay, no glasses on #TheView. Either way she looks nice. She has really good skin," one fan tweeted.
