Amy Winehouse actress Marisa Abela seen screaming during filming of Blake Fielder-Civil's arrest

 Posted on March 18, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  136 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

SCREAMING actress Marisa Abela is held back by police officers as she plays Amy Winehouse in scenes filmed outside the late singer’s old flat.

Marisa, 26, was recreating the moment the star’s then-boyfriend Blake Fielder-Civil was arrested.

Amy had followed Blake into the street after he was picked up by police over an attempt to stop his GBH trial in 2007.

The scenes, filmed in Camden, North London, show Amy chasing him and shouting: “I want to go with him. Baby, I love you. Baby, I’ll be fine.”

Blake, played by SAS Rogue Heroes star Jack O’Connell, is seen being led away in handcuffs.

The biopic, named after Amy’s second album Back to Black, has upset some friends of the star, left in Camden and right with Blake, but is backed by the Winehouse estate.
