Stormi Webster's Most Fashionable, Adorable Outfits

 Posted on April 19, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  403 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Like her mama! Stormi Webster is following in Kylie Jenner’s footsteps and shaping up to be quite the fashionista. The 4-year-old has established quite the cool girl aesthetic over the years, rocking everything from sneakers and baggy jeans to princess dresses. 

Plus, with a designer wardrobe and handbag collection that’s objectively out of this world, it’s no surprise that the little one has picked up a flair for fashion. She’s able to accessorize her looks with Balenciaga, Prada or Louis Vuitton and more. — and she loves every second of it. 

Case in point: Back in April 2019, the Kylie Cosmetics founder posted a video to her Instagram feed of Stormi carrying a little pink Birkin bag with her crochet onesie and gold sandals. “She won’t let go of the purse,” Jenner says in the background. Sounds like she knows good style when she sees it!

Although Stormi almost always looks the part on her own, the best is when the mother-daughter duo deliver epic twinning moments or color coordinated ensembles.

From champagne sparkles at the 2018 Christmas celebrations to the ruffled cerulean look in August 2020, they never disappoint. Sometimes Stormi even ups her own style game, adding a cute little accessory. 

For instance, in June 2019 the Lip Kit creator posted a mirror selfie of the two of them wearing very similar tie-dye outfits. Instead of matching her mom, Stormi’s ensemble featured an unattached sleeve. Because, why not?

Stormi has even directly mimicked her mom. For 2019 Halloween, she straight up recreated Jenner’s Met Gala look. From the purple Versace dress to the lilac wig, the little one couldn’t have been cuter. 

The big sister to be doesn’t just rock casual clothes and serve up street style inspo for tots, she also has some serious red carpet style. She made an appearance in NYC in June 2021 at the Parsons Benefit. 

Stormi, who wore a little black dress and Nike kicks, posed with her parents like a total pro. There was no denying that she was hand-down the star of the evening.
