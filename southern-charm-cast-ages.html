'Southern Charm' Cast Ages Here's What We Know

 Posted on May 29, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  571 words  |  Aldo Pusey

How old is the cast of 'Southern Charm'? Here's a breakdown of the cast's ages and more! Read for details on the Bravo reality series.

When we first met the cast of Southern Charm in 2014, they were just a rambunctious group of younger adults in Charleston, S.C. with big dreams. Fast forward to now and the reality stars are all grown up — well, at least when it comes to their ages, and not necessarily their maturity levels.

How old is the cast now? Here's a breakdown of their ages.

Craig Conover — 34

Born to parents Martha Foster Conover and Craig Conover Sr., Craig Conover Jr. is from Fenwick Island, Del. The 34-year-old reality star has been around since the series premiere.

Austen Kroll — 36

Austen Kroll, 36, made his debut in Season 4 of Southern Charm. Since joining the cast, Austen has developed a reputation of being a ladies' man. In the past, he's been romantically linked to several of his co-stars — including, but not limited to, Summer House stars Ciara Miller and Lindsay Hubbard, and Southern Charm’s Madison LeCroy.

Madison LeCroy — 32

Greenville, S.C. native Madison LeCroy is 32. Following her messy breakup with Austen, Madison found love with her new husband Brett Randle. He proposed to the reality star in October 2021, after seven months of dating. She shares a young son with her ex-husband, Josh Hughes.

Shep Rose — 43

As one of the eldest in the crew, 43-year-old Shep is rarely the voice of reason. But with age, comes wisdom... right? In Season 9, Shep is back from a sabbatical in Australia following his breakup with Taylor Ann Green and trying to navigate how to be her friend.

Leva Bonaparte — 44

Leva Bonaparte joined the cast of Southern Charm alongside her husband, Lamar, in Season 7. The 44-year-old was born in India and raised in Canada and Bolivia.

Venita Aspen — 29

Venita Aspen, 29, was introduced to viewers by fellow Southern Charm newcomer Leva and joined the cast as a full-time cast member in Season 8.

Olivia Flowers — 31

Olivia debuted on the show in Season 8, when she was dating fellow Southern Charm star Austen Kroll. Now, she's moved on and is reportedly dating other people — including newcomer Rod Razavi.

Taylor Ann Green — 28

Taylor is the youngest cast member on the show and was previously linked to longtime cast member Shep Rose, who is more than 10 years her senior.

Rodrigo Reyes — 39

Interior decorator and floral designer officially joins the cast for Season 9, but his friendships with his fellow stars go way back. Rodrigo has been with his boyfriend Tyler for several years, too.

Rod Razavi — 40

Rod is another newcomer this season. He's pursuing Olivia (even though Austen might have some objections to that). Rod has a background in technology and specializes in database programming. He has worked for several nonprofits.

Jarrett "JT" Thomas — 38

Jarrett "JT" Thomas is the third and final newcomer to Season 9. He's originally from Virginia and works in real estate — but don't let the professional job title fool you. He's also super into partying.
