Brody Jenner, Josie Canseco Party in Hollywood Amid Dating Rumors

 Posted on July 11, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  372 words  |  Larita Shotwell

A budding romance? Brody Jenner and Josie Canseco publicly stepped out together for the first time since rumors of their potential new relationship began to swirl.

Playboy’s former Playmate of the Month, 22, sported a white crop top with black ripped jeans and wore her blonde hair in a high ponytail as she and the reality star, 35, arrived at Warwick nightclub in Hollywood on Wednesday, August 14. Jenner went casual in an all-black ensemble that he paired with a baseball hat.

According to a source, Jenner arrived at the club with Canseco and several pals around 12 a.m. “Brody and Josie weren’t touchy feely, but she spent the night out by his side and they seemed comfortable together,” the source told Us Weekly, noting that Canseco “seemed like she was into” the DJ.

“He seemed happy to be with another girl after all the news surrounding his breakup,” the source added, referring to his split from ex Kaitlynn Carter, who was spotted kissing Miley Cyrus poolside in Italy on Saturday, August 10.

“Brody doesn’t care about Kaitlynn and Miley hooking up. He‘s in a good space and he doesn’t have any somberness relating to his breakup with her,” the source explained. “It was a long time coming and he knows that. He also thinks Kaitlynn is milking the attention.”

The Hills: New Beginnings star first sparked speculation he was dating Canseco earlier this month. Jenner posted an Instagram photo of himself in Malibu on Sunday, August 11, to which Canseco commented with the heart-eyes emoji. The MTV personality’s mom, Linda Thompson, replied to Canseco’s comment with two kiss-face emojis.

TMZ later reported that the pair met during the Hills cast’s recent press tour in New York City, and have since been hanging out regularly, in addition to showing PDA around their pals.
