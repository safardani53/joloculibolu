Armie, 35, was seen in California taking a cell phone call while in a blue patterned bathing suit - readjusting to life in the U.S.A.

He was spotted poolside at an unknown hotel after spending much of the past two years in the Cayman Islands.

SHOCK DOC

In the soon-to-come documentary, Courtney Vucekovich and other alleged victims speak out against the Call Me By Your Name actor.

On August 10, 2022, the official trailer for House of Hammer debuted online.

Discovery+ summarized the project: "Debauchery. Deceit. Abuse. Addiction. Corruption.

"Over the course of five generations, the men in the Hammer family have more secrets and scandals than any vault can contain.

"Armie Hammer’s alleged crimes are only the tip of the iceberg."

The description further reads: "Through a trove of archives and interviews from survivors and family members, the dark and twisted secrets of the Hammer family come to light."

On Friday, September 2, audiences can stream the House of Hammer docuseries when it premieres on Discovery+.

In the trailer, two of Hammer’s exes, Courtney and Julia Morrison, recant their experiences with the disgraced star.

They also shared troubling messages they alleged are from him.

"In the beginning, I felt like this was all perfect; this was amazing," Vucekovich said in the trailer.

"But then things changed. He pushes your boundaries a little bit at a time. You’re his, completely … I mean, he said, ‘I’m 100% a cannibal.' I’m freaking out."

The streaming service's President of Investigative Content Jason Sarlanis said in a statement: "With House Of Hammer, we witness truly disturbing details and sinister secrets that money and power couldn’t hide forever.

"This documentary provides an important platform for the incredibly courageous women who came forward to share their stories.

"We hope their courage inspires others to continue meaningful conversations around abuse in our society."

TERROR TEXTS

On January 11, 2021, Instagram messages allegedly sent by Armie Hammer leaked on social media.

In the social media exchanges, the Death on The Nile star supposedly claimed he is "100 percent a cannibal" and that he wanted to "eat" the receiving party, as well as "drink" the reported victim's blood.

The texts went on to include remarks of a cannibalistic nature and stated the sender's desire to feed on the person at the receiving end of the exchange.

In the wake of the messages, several more women spoke out against Armie Hammer, including his ex-girlfriends Paige Lorenze and Courtney.

His ex-wife Elizabeth Chambers and aunt Casey Hammer also sided against him.

Due to the collective complaints of sexual abuse that were reported against Armie, he lost out on a number of movie and TV roles.

He was ousted from the Jennifer Lopez romantic comedy Shotgun Wedding, as well as the Julia Roberts and Sean Penn-led Watergate drama Gaslit.

Hammer's wife Elizabeth Chambers filed for divorce in 2020, citing irreconcilable differences.

The actor spent nine months in a rehabilitation center after being accused of abuse by a number of women. 

Earlier this month, Hammer resurfaced with Elizabeth and their family in Los Angeles amid scrutiny questioning around his work.
