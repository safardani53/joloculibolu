Amber Rose Wears Gigantic Sunglasses to Billboard Music Awards: Pic

 Posted on August 24, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  215 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Just like Pharrell Williams' hat was the talk of the Grammys red carpet, Amber Rose's sunglasses stole the show at the 2014 Billboard Music Awards on Sunday, May 18. 

The massive shades covered more than half of the model-actress' face, spanning from above her eyebrows to the top of her lips. Many on Twitter compared them to the face-covering visor worn by disgraced former Clippers owner Donald Sterling's ex-girlfriend V. Stiviano.

Posing with husband Wiz Khalifa, Rose wore an all black ensemble with a blazer, combat boots, and even black lipstick. Her rapper beau matched in a white leather coat, white button-up shirt, and ripped black jeans. 

The couple welcomed son Sebastian in February 2013 and married in July 2013.
