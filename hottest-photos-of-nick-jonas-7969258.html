Hot Photos of Nick Jonas to Celebrate His Birthday

 Posted on September 22, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  402 words  |  Larita Shotwell

From teen heartthrob to devoted father, Nick Jonas has been "Burnin' Up" the hearts of fans for years.

The multi-hyphenate, who is currently on tour with his brothers, is officially 31 on Sept. 16, 2023, and to celebrate, we thought there was no better way than to look back at some of his hottest photos to date.

From red carpets to serenading stadiums full of adoring fans, these are some of the birthday boy's hottest looks.

Changing It Up

While the OG fans fell in love with his famous curls, a few years back Nick opted for a different look when he buzzed his hair off. But that's okay, he can really pull off any hairstyle.

Moves Like Jonas

Not only can he sing, he is not afraid to show off his dance moves once he hits the stage.

Leading Man

And while he certainly burns up the stage, he sure knows how to rock a red carpet as well.

Big Fan

Oh, and he's passionate about sports! While his brother, Joe, stayed cool and collected at his side, Nick couldn't contain his excitement at a basketball game between the New York Knicks and Miami Heat.

Lover Not a Fighter

For his role as an MMA fighter on the series Kingdom, from 2014 to 2017, Nick said he "put on about 20 lbs. of muscle in a month and a half" — and this photo sure shows it.

Getting in Character

For his role in 2021's Chaos Walking, his costuming was a bit more ... covered up.

Almost Showtime

Nothing is more "Cool" than a guy taking it all in ahead of playing a show at Chicago's Wrigley Field.

Hats Are Definitely Allowed

IYKYK. Nick Jonas in a backwards hat. Enough said.

On-Stage Smolder

The perfect photo does not exist — just kidding! We found it. The lighting, the twinkle in his eyes, what more can you ask for.

Cheers to the Entrepreneur

And did we mention he has his own brand of tequila? What can't this man do!
