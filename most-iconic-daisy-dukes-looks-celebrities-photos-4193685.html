Jessica Simpson &amp; More In The Trend Hollywood Life

 Posted on June 9, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  801 words  |  Aldo Pusey

There’s nothing like a reliable pair of denim short shorts — also known as Daisy Dukes! And no one knows that better than these fierce and fabulous celebs. So many stars have been rocking Daisy Dukes not just during the summer of 2020, but also for decades! The look has grown in popularity over the years, and now, we’re taking a look at the stars who keep the fashion statement going!

Celebs like Jessica Simpson and Hailey Baldwin love a Daisy Duke look. In fact, they’ve been spotted out and about in the short shorts a lot this summer. But they aren’t the only ones rocking the style and showing off their long legs with all of the confidence in the world. Check out the photos of these celebs and more in Daisy Dukes!

Jessica Simpson

Jessica Simpson might just be the undisputed queen of Daisy Dukes, and long may she reign! The actress gave the look a whole new spin when she sported the iconic denim short shorts in the 2005 film The Dukes of Hazzard where she starred as none other than Daisy Duke! Over the years, Jessica has gone through a total evolution, but the one element of her style that has remained the same is just how much she adores the denim short shorts.

“It’s just something that’s authentically a part of me,” Jessica shared EXCLUSIVELY with HollywoodLife. “Even before I was Daisy, I wore short shorts that were cut off. I would make them in high school by cutting my jeans. So, it’s kind of one of those things like overalls, for me, I do the same thing. I have overalls from when I was young and I still wear them. It’s like comfort clothes.” We cannot wait to see her continue to rock the look in the future!

Hailey Baldwin

Hailey Baldwin has also proven that she is ready to take the crown as the new Daisy Dukes queen. The stunning model has been spotted out and about on the reg sporting the little shorts, where she’s shown that her long legs can go on for days. Mrs. Bieber has worn these shorts for various occasions, proving their versatility.

She’s been seen wearing them out to a romantic date with her hubs, Justin Bieber, while running errands, and even while filming a music video — like in the photo above. It’s so clear that these short shorts are a go-to look for Hailey, and even though the seasons are changing and it’s getting chillier outside, we cannot wait until it warms up again and for Hailey to put her own unique twist on the iconic garment!

Cindy Crawford

Age is merely a number, and no one proves that more often that legendary model Cindy Crawford. The total stunner looked super laid back when she was spotted out and about with her daughter, model Kaia Gerber, rocking a soft cotton striped shirt and a pair of denim Daisy Dukes! Cindy looked like the epitome of a California girl, wearing her look with total effortlessness.

During the height of her career, Cindy was the total girl next door. She rocked easy looks that were replicated by women across the world, and pulled it all off with total expertise. Even as she’s matured, Cindy has still been a denim queen, appearing on the cover of magazines from time to time, like her 2018 Town & Country cover, and giving her fans life with her denim looks!

Miley Cyrus

Miley Cyrus‘s music has evolved so much over the years. But she’s shown that though she’s a pop princess, she’s also a country gal at heart. The “Midnight Sky” singer loves sporting Daisy Dukes any chance she gets — and we don’t blame her! Often, Miley loves to play up her looks with fabulous takes that reflect her personality. With these Daisy Dukes, Miley added a lot of color with her Dior T-shirt and pink and peach sneakers.

Kendall Jenner

While we’re at it, let’s add Kendall Jenner to the list of supermodels who love a pair of Daisy Dukes. Kendall is one of the most sought-after supermodels in the world. With her stunning looks, she can pull off almost any style, including casual short shorts. Here, Kendall enjoyed some downtime at the beach and looked absolutely picture perfect in her bikini top and denim Daisy Dukes!

Sofia Richie

Finally, Sofia Richie has been the unassailable Daisy Dukes queen of summer 2020. She’s been spotted out and about rocking the look throughout the season. Sofia has given her Daisy Dukes a vintage spin by rocking a total ‘90s-inspired ensemble, or dressing it up with some high-end accessories! Given the emotionally intense summer she’s had — with her breakup from Scott Disick after three years of dating — it’s been great to see her looking her best despite working through so much.
