KHLOÉ Kardashian is always seen with her sidekick and BFF Malika Haqq.

The 39-year-old TV personality considers Malika and her twin sister, Khadijah, as her other set of sisters.

Who is Khloé Kardashian’s best friend, Malika Haqq?

Malika Haqq and Khloé Kardashian met at the age of 15 when the twin was dating one of Khloé's high school best friends.

Khloé reminisced on their meeting saying: “Malika was dating one of my male best friends. He would always keep us apart because I am sure he knew we would just hit it off,” she wrote on her blog.

“One day we met and we did. Malika and Khadijah have been my best friends ever since.”

Malika and her sister, Khadijah, were born in Los Angeles, California, on March 10, 1983. Their parents immigrated from the Khuzestan Province in Iran.

The twins had older twin sisters, one of whom died at the age of 18. They also have a brother, Jamal.

Malika was a staple on Keeping Up With the Kardashians and their spin-off shows since they began in 2007.

She has amassed her own following and has become an influencer herself booking deals with brands like What Waist, Pretty Little Thing, Bio-Oil, and Naked Wardrobe.

Malika is listed as an actress with 12 credits on IMDB but is really only recognized for her role in Disney's 2005 action-comedy, Sky High where she played, Penny, alongside Khadijah, as the cheerleading captain who can multiply herself to cheer or fight her enemies.

Have Khloé and Malika worked together?

At the beginning of Keeping Up With the Kardashians, Malika had a stint as Khloé's personal assistant but that didn't last long when she wanted to get back into modeling.

In 2015, E! tried out a series starring Malika and Khadijah which followed employees, or "dolls", at one of the Kardashians’ Dash clothing boutiques where the twins were supposedly co-managing the store, however, Dash Dolls was canceled after just eight episodes.

Khloé and Malika are constantly working together by showing up on each other's Instagram ads and supporting each other at promotional events.

They officially teamed up in 2019, releasing a limited edition BFF Collection with BECCA Cosmetics creating a set that included a bronzer, blush, glow palette, lipsticks, and highlighters.

Talking about working on their collection Khloé said: "We know our strengths and our weaknesses — who is stronger at what — and that's a blessing of a real friendship. We're not competitive with one another. We encourage one another."

When asked about the secret to their long-lasting friendship Malika said it was based on their "honesty," and while Khloé echoed that thought, she also credited their "open lines of communication."

Does Malika Haqq have children?

Malika gave birth to her first child, Ace Flores, on March 14, 2020.

She shares her three-year-old son with ex-boyfriend and rapper O.T. Genasis.
