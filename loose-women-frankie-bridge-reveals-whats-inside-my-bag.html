Frankie Bridge shows 'what's in my bag' including Christmas snack &amp; item she had to stop husband Way

HAVE you ever wondered what the famous faces from TV carry around in their handbags?

Now singer turned presenter Frankie Bridge has revealed what she stores in hers – and it’s not all as glamorous as you might think.

As part of the TikTok trend ‘What’s In My Bag, The Saturdays’ Frankie, 34, decided to give her fans a glimpse into her belongings, which she described as her “whole life in one little bag”. 

The Loose Women panellist explained in a video posted to her account: “So I’ve seen a lot of those what’s in my bag? So I thought I’d do one for you.”

She then opened her large black bag from brand Dragon Diffusion, before admitting that maybe she should have cleared it out before hitting record.

But, true to her word, Frankie then started to go through her belongings.

The mum-of-two, who is married to former footballer Wayne Bridge, started out by pulling out a portable keep cup.

She explained: “I take this every morning when I’m doing Loose Women. I hate any plastic-y taste and the metal ones I find keep the tea too hot.”

Next was another travel essential that Frankie said she’d come to rely on. 

She continued: “I’m always doing long journeys, so I’ve got my headphones.

“And I’ve had to put my name on them because Wayne kept stealing them and then we kept getting them mixed up – so now he cannot get away with it.”

Revealing what was a fan of listening to, Frankie continued: “At the minute, I’m listening to the Diary of a CEO podcast [with] Steven Bartlett.”

But she said her AirPods also had an extra hidden use. 

“I also put them in when I just can’t be bothered to talk to anyone – it’s great.”

The third item that Frankie showed was one that she said she wasn’t sure whether she wanted to admit to or not.

She rummaged around and then picked out a small item wrapped in tin foil.

She confessed: “I’ve got a mince pie in my bag – it’s mince pie season as far as I’m concerned.

“Breakfast used to be my favourite meal of the day, but now I really struggle. So just a cheeky little mince pie every so often always helps to bridge the gap.”

Frankie then shared how she carried some beauty essentials with her each day, including  a trusty lip balm by brand Tatcha to prevent dry lips and a lip gloss from her Space NK advent calendar that she has already opened.
