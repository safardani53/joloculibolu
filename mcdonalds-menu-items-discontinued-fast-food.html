Four McDonald's menu items you'll never see again and customers are 'dreaming' of their return

 Posted on May 4, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  504 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

MCDONALD’S is a staple in many households across the world - but not all favorites last and customers missing four items from the brand in particular.

Fans have gotten used to ordering their cherished meals from the fast-food giant.

However, every once in a while, McDonald’s will try something new to spark excitement for customers.

Some people might not be impressed, but others fall in love with new food additions.

Meanwhile, sometimes McDonald’s chooses to ax a new food addition due to a number of reasons.

Whether it's a cost or demand issue, some menu items just don't stack up.

Below, we’ve put together a list of fan favorites that customers are still craving.

1. FRUIT 'N YOGURT PARFAIT

This breakfast staple was a healthier alternative to many of the greasy offerings McDonald's offered.

Served all day, the parfait came with low-fat yogurt, sliced strawberries and blueberries, and crunchy granola.

Initially offered on the popular dollar menu, prices rose as high as $1.19 before being discontinued, according to So Loyal.

Popular Twitter account Discontinued Foods! posted a photo of the discontinued menu item, stating it was available from 1999 to 2020.

While it has yet to be confirmed, the parfait was likely given the ax during the pandemic as companies phased out some lower-performing menu items.

2. MUSHROOM SWISS BURGER

Another popular item to get the ax was the Mushroom Swiss Burger.

This was part of the Signature Crafted Recipes line.

The burger came with a quarter-pound fresh-beef patty, seasoned mushrooms, creamy bistro aioli, Swiss cheese, and crispy onion strings on a toasted artisan roll.

The chain discontinued the burger as it moved away from craft burger options to focus on the traditional quarter pounder.

While it's been gone for quite some time, customers are still missing it.

One fan to Twitter and wrote: "Daydreaming about the old school McDonald’s Mushroom and Swiss Burger."

3. SNACK WRAP

The McDonald's Snack Wrap was another item that played tug of war with the joint, as it was on and off menus multiple times.

However, it didn't perform well enough to make it to the permanent menu.

Before being discontinued back in 2016, the wrap included chicken, shredded lettuce, and cheese, all housed in a warm tortilla.

Sadly, the company thought it was too complex and decided to give it the boot.

4. MIGHTY WINGS

While McDonald's has the mega-popular chicken nuggets, it also tried its hand at chicken wings.

The bone-in chicken wings were coated in spicy breading and served with a side of spicy buffalo sauce.

Sold from 1990 to 2004, the fast food joint added them back from 2013 to 2014.

Customers could order either a three, five, or a 10-piece meal.

Fast foodies still miss the meal and even started a petition to get them back, racking up 784 signatures.
