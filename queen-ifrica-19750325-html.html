Ventrice Morgan, whose stage name is Queen Ifrica, is a reggae singer and disc jockey from the hills above Montego Bay, Jamaica. She is the daughter of ska music legend Derrick Morgan but was raised by her mother and stepfather. A Rasta by faith, she is known for her work in the community and for writing songs about deeply personal subject matter in songs such as "Below the Waist" and "Daddy".
