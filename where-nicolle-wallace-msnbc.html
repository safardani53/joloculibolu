Why is Nicolle Wallace not on MSNBC today?

 Posted on April 21, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  527 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

NICOLLE Wallace is widely known for her hosting duties on MSNBC's Deadline: White House.

As of November 6, 2023, the famed host went absent from the network, leaving fans wondering where she was.

Why is Nicolle Wallace not on MSNBC today?

As fans tuned into Deadline: White House on Monday, November 20, 2023, many were shocked to see Alicia Menendez taking the place of Nicolle Wallace's hosting duties.

This absence marked the second week in a row that Nicolle had been missing from TV screens.

Wallace was also missing from the show on Tuesday, November 21, however, Deadline posted on X, formerly known as Twitter, that she would be back "with a very special announcement" sometime within the 5 pm hour.

Calling into the show for her appearance, Wallace revealed that she just welcomed a daughter named Isabella to her family.

Wallace said she was "smitten" with the new addition and confirmed she was able to keep the baby a secret as she was never pregnant.

"There are pros and cons to being a mom again at 51," she added. "But it's a blessing to be focused again on what has always grounded me and that is my beautiful family."

On December 4, 2023, Wallace surprised fans by posting an update on X: "I will be back tomorrow for an important conversation—one day only! (Then back to my family!) Hope to see you there!"

Her one-day return to MSNBC comes after two weeks of being off the air.

On December 5, Wallace delighted fans by returning to Deadline to interview Liz Cheney.

Is Nicolle Wallace married?

Wallace is married to MSNBC contributor Michael Schmidt.

The two tied the knot in an intimate ceremony on April 2, 2022.

The pair met on her show as he was a frequent guest. In March 2019, it was revealed the two had begun dating.

This news comes after her divorce from her first husband, Mark Wallace.
