Who is Ivy Paige? The Voice UK 2018 contestant and burlesque showgirl in Team Olly Murs

 Posted on May 8, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  390 words  |  Aldo Pusey

IVY Paige is a burlesque showgirl who is appearing on the 2018 season of ITV's hit talent show The Voice.

But, what else do we know about Ivy, and where does she perform?

Ivy began her burlesque show in 2006 after graduating from Dartington College of Arts with a first-class degree.

On her official website, Ivy describes herself as an international showgirl, singer, chanteuse and burlesque star who has "wowed audiences across the globe".

She performed the first ever Burlesque show in The Middle East and hosted the smash hit European Tour, An Evening of Burlesque.

Paige has performed at The London Burlesque Festival, The Hurly Burly Show, An Evening of Burlesque and the Edinburgh Fringe Festival, The Hammersmith Apollo and Shepherds Bush Empire where she was the support artiste to John Waters and The Puppini Sisters on their respective UK tours.

More from the voice 2018

She has presented and sang for BBC presents Cabaret at The Edinburgh Festival and has filmed From Burlesque Queen to Body Builder, a documentary for BBC 1 which will air in 2018.

Ivy has had her own show in London's West End at The Leicester Square Theatre and at the Matcham Room at The Hippodrome Casino

Ivy has also been featured in the numerous publications and press including The Mail on Sunday, The Guardian, The Sun, Love it Magazine, BBC, Time Out, and Cosmopolitan Magazine.

Thanks to the fame of American performer Dita Von Teese, the world of burlesque has become more popular and accessible in recent years - but Ivy says she doesn't like to copy her peers, and always adds individual element to her performances.

She has said: "When people think of burlesque, I think Dita is often the person they think of.

"But I didn’t start doing this because of her, I have always been a showgirl at heart so it just seemed like the natural thing to do.

"Some people are shocked when I tell them what I do, but most people are just interested in it.

"I think most people know what it is but haven’t been to see it, but they should."

In her audition on The Voice, Ivy performs "Why Don't You Do Right" by Peggy Lee.

As you'd expect from a self-proclaimed international showgirl, Ivy is on social media.

She also has her own website, www.ivypaige.com.
