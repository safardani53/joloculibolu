Mans genius way for eating corn on the cob without any mess goes viral

 Posted on May 30, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  186 words  |  Martina Birk

CORN on the cob may be delicious but we have to admit it's a total nightmare to eat.

Whether it's bits of sweetcorn stuck between your teeth or butter dripping down your hands, it has to be up there with spag bol as one of the messiest meals around.

But all that could be about to change.

One man's genius hack for eating corn on the cob has gone viral and it's going to revolutionise your mealtimes.

Not only will it reduce mess but it makes sure you get every last piece of corn - and it couldn't be simpler.

Simply get a skewer and slide it along a row of corn, making sure you pierce every piece as you go.

The corn should come away cleanly when you lift it up and you then simply eat off the skewer for a mess-free meal.
