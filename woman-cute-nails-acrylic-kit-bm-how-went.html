I wanted cute nails on the cheap so tried out the 4.99 acrylic kit from B&amp;M - here's how it went

 Posted on January 1, 0001  |  2 minutes  |  269 words  |  Aldo Pusey

A WOMAN who didn't want to shell out for a set of new nails at the beauty salon has shared how she did her own for just £4.99.

Elliemay took to TikTok to post a video showcasing how she used the kit, giving herself acrylics for the very first time.

She began by removing the remaining remnants of her glittery varnish, before giving her nails and cuticles a tidy up.

She then applied the bright white tips, before cutting them down and applying the acrylic liquid over the top.

Elliemay then gently buffed the surface of the nails with a file, before painting them - teal and silver glitter.

"First time doing acrylics and tried the £5 acrylic nail kit from b&ms!" she captioned her video.
