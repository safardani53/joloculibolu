Interest and Affairs of Travis

Fimmel loves camping, surfing, riding motorbikes, and going to beaches. His favorite television character is Tony Soprano and his favorite brand is L' Oreal.

After a few unsuccessful relationships with Mei Melancon, Merrin Dungey, Rachel Hunter, Jessica Miller, Nicole Appleton and Ceara Lynch the actor is now single and focusing on his career. Travis affair with Merrin Dungey lasted just for a year. He was also rumored to be dating his co-star Paula Patton and though the fans were hoping for their marriage they separated after a year.

Similarly his affair with Jessica Miller was loved by the fans and were so eager to see them as a married couple but unfortunately, the relationship lasted for short duration as well. There were rumors regarding Travis being gay as well which later proved to be false.
