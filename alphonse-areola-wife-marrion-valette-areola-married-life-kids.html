The two-time World Cup winner Alphonse Areola has been happily married to wife Marrion Valette Areola since 2014.

Alphonse and Marrion are proud parents to three daughters.

The French goalkeeper Alphonse Francis Areola plays for the national team and West Ham United in the Premier League.

Areola is the product of French powerhouses PSG. He spent most of his initial days on loan across France and Spain.

Moreover, Areola made his senior debut for PSG on 18 May 2013, replacing first-choice goalie Salvatore Sirigu.

He won several titles in the French league. He was loaned to Real Madrid for 2019-20, where he won La Liga.

Likewise, Alphonse has won the World Cup twice with France, once in 2018 Russia, and the other time with the U-20 Youth team.

In 2013, he was dubbed the ‘hero without a cape’ for France as he saved two spot-kicks to help his U-20 team win the Fifa World Cup.

Furthermore, he signed permanently with West Ham United on June 27, 2022. The deal was worth around €12 million.

Recently, he saved Enzo Fernandez’s penalty in the Hammer’s 3-1 victory against Chelsea.

Alphonse Areola Wife Marrion Valette Areola: Married Life & Kids

Alphonse Back In David Moyes’ Faith

In Sunday’s derby with Chelsea, Alphonse Areola was given a chance ahead of West Ham United’s No. 1 Lukasz Fabianski.

Areola justified his decision to David Moyes’ to make him the first-choice goalie.

Furthermore, the Frenchman has had to wait for his opportunity, which he did patiently, and his game against The Blues was of a different caliber.
