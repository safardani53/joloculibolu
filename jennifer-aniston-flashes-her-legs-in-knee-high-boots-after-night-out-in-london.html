JENNIFER Aniston looked simply spectacular as she made her way back to her hotel after a night out in London.

The Friends actress flashed her legs as she got into her car after enjoying an evening at The Chiltern Firehouse.

Jennifer, 47, looked great in a black mini-dress which she teamed with matching knee-high boots.

A dark jacket with a flash of white and red at the sleeves competed her outfit.

The star had earlier appeared on The One Show to promote her latest film Office Christmas Party.

But minutes before sharing the sofa with the BBC programme's inspirational Rickshaw team - a group of disabled children - the screen favourite mentioned "sex toys" live on air.

It all got very awkward very quickly, as the film’s director Josh Gordon compared her new character to her Horrible Bosses and Friends roles, as the kids stood nearby.

While he admitted she was “a little bit of both”,  Jennifer agreed it was a similar role, only “just without the sex toys”.

Luckily, host Alex Jones laughed the remark off, while Matt Baker quickly said: “There we are, is that a fair assessment?”
