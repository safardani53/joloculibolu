SOPHIA Urista is a singer and songwriter who fronts the punk protest band, Brass Against.

She made headlines after an incident at a concert in which she urinated on a fan.

Who are Sophia Urista's parents?

Sophia Urista has not shared much information about her personal life in regards to her upbringing.

Not much is known about her parents. She has suggested before that when she originally moved to New York at the age of 21, she had $700 to her name and no support from anyone, her parents included.

Sophia has featured her mother on her Instagram profile multiple times. Her mom, Alicia, has an account under the name of shilohnutrition that reveals she is a massage therapist and wellness coach.

In 2020, Sophia posted photo of her as a baby being held by her mother, wishing her mom a happy 57th birthday.

For the caption, she wrote:

"Thank you for introducing me to my world of music, cooking, self-love and care, & how to work a room!! Thank you for your unconditional love, your unrelenting and naive charm, your strong willed nature, and your laughter.

"You’re my best friend and I’m always blown away by your love for me."

In 2021, Sophia's mother also appeared on her Instagram-based cooking show, Ooo, Mami!. Sophia and her fiance, Jess King, post videos of them cooking various recipes.

Alicia was the star of their episode about burgers, offering up a gluten free option made with collard greens.

No information about Sophia Urista's father has been made available online.

Is Sophia Urista married?

Sophia Urista is engaged to Peloton instructor and social media personality, Jess King.

The two met in 2015, while Sophia was working at The Box, a cabaret nightclub in New York City.

“I just felt overcome and overwhelmed by this pull and draw towards her," King told POPSUGAR.

"And I walked up to her and said something totally ridiculous. Something like, 'Hey, do you want to hook up?'"

Although it was King's first time with a woman, the two quickly got serious, and have been dating ever since.

After spending quarantine with each other, Sophia proposed to King at the premiere of her music video Everything About You. The song was written about their relationship, and the video debuted at a private celebaration with a group of close friends.

Sophia projected the video onto a set of white sheets, on a terrace wall in her best friend's garden. She then popped the question, to which King immediately said yes.

There isn't yet a set date for their wedding, but the two have discussed their desire for a family together.

After years of being a performing artist, Sophia Urista has managed to rack up a high net worth.

Her fiance, Jess King, has an estimated net worth of around $1.6million, according to NetWorthSpot.
