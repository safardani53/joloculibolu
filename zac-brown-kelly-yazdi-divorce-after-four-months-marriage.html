ZAC Brown and his wife, Kelly Yazdi, have officially called it quits.

The couple - who tied the knot in August - shared a joint statement regarding their relationship.

"We are in the process of divorce. Our mutual respect for one another remains. We wish each other the best and will always appreciate our time together," the ex-couple told TMZ.

"As we navigate this personal matter, we simply request privacy during this time."

On August 31, the news outlet revealed that Zac, 45, and Kelly, 32, got married in a secret ceremony in Coweta County, Georgia.

The couple were engaged for about a year after the model was spotted wearing a large ring on her finger.

MORE ABOUT ZAC BROWN & HIS BAND

Zac was born on July 31, 1978 in Atlanta, Georgia.

The creation of the Zac Brown Band started in 2002 - and in 2004, they released their first album called Home Grown.

The band currently has nine members in its ensemble.

The country artist is the lead vocalist and guitarist.

He's joined by Jimmy De Martini, who provides backup vocals and plays the fiddle.

John Driskell Hopkins plays bass guitar, guitar, baritone guitar, banjo, ukulele, upright bass, and provides backup vocals for the band.

Coy Bowles plays the guitar and keyboards.

Chris Fryar is the band's drummer.

Clay Cook provides backup vocals and plays guitar, keyboards, mandolin, and the steel guitar.

Matt Mangano is the band's bass guitarist.

Daniel de los Reyes is the band's percussionist.

Caroline Jones, the band's first female member, provides backup vocals and plays the guitar, keyboards, and harmonica.

THEIR MUSIC

The band has released seven studio albums, two live albums, one greatest hits album, and two extended plays.

Their biggest hit includes Chicken Fried - which has over 640million streams on Spotify.

Other songs include Knee Deep featuring Jimmy Buffet, Colder Weather, Whatever It Is, Toes, Homegrown, Free, Loving You Easy, and Highway 20 Ride.

Throughout their music career, the country band was nominated for eight Grammys and won three - including Best Country Album in 2013.
