Who is HGTV star Hilary Farr and when did she leave Love It or List It?

 Posted on January 1, 0001  |  3 minutes  |  615 words  |  Martina Birk

HILARY Farr is a designer, entrepreneur, television host, and former actress of British and Canadian descent.

She is best known for co-hosting the HGTV series Love It or List It with David Visentin.

Who is Hilary Farr?

Hilary Farr is a British-Canadian home designer, TV personality, and former actor. 

She was born and raised in Toronto, Canada, where she first started to hone in on her artistic talents.

Her mother was a traveler and a home decorator, which led Farr to follow in her footsteps.

Farr was also interested in the performing arts as a child and took part in theater.

Throughout the 1970s and 1980s, Farr appeared in several films and television shows, including The Rocky Horror Picture Show, according to her IMDb page.

Her other projects include small roles in films such as Sex Farm, Layout for 5 Models, and Never Mind the Quality, Feel the Width.

Farr's professional career began when she relocated to Los Angeles and started renovating homes.

Farr has mostly worked as an interior designer in Australia, the UK, and the US since then.

Since 2008, Farr has co-hosted the HGTV series, Love It or List It with realtor David Visentin.

Farr and Visentin have helped families transform their homes for 19 seasons.

What did Hilary Farr say about leaving Love It or List It?

The co-host of HGTV's Love It or List It is ready to move on.

On December 1, 2023, Farr revealed that she would be leaving her longtime gig.

In an exclusive statement to People, Farr revealed that she would be leaving the show that "got me through hard times."

"It's got the audiences through hard times. It has evolved into a mainstay of people's lives and it's been incredibly gratifying," she said.

"But now it's time for me to move on and meet new challenges."

The designer has appeared in 206 episodes since the show's debut in 2008.

Farr continued: "This show has encompassed a divorce, cancer, my son getting married, my son having three children, my long-term relationship breaking up."

Besides co-hosting Love It or List It for over 15 years, Farr also hosted two seasons of HGTV's Tough Love with Hilary Farr.

Farr also added that she'll miss "everything" about her co-host.

“We are who we are [in real life] on that show,” she adds.

“Think of your brother as his most obnoxious and annoying, that's David.”

The star also added that the duo's friendship "is not going to just disappear because we're not working together.”

When asked whether the show would continue without her, and with a new co-host, Farr said she didn't know, but she "wouldn't be surprised."

“I wish them all the luck in the world,” she said, referring to her longtime co-workers at HGTV.

“And I would probably tune in and watch it," she added, referring to the show continuing without her.

What is Hilary Farr’s net worth?

Farr has amassed a hefty chunk of change thanks in part to her decades-long career on reality television.

Her net worth is reportedly around $8 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. 

Farr is also the president of Hilary Farr Designs.

The brand has furniture, rugs, bedding, lighting, and more, according to her website, "with a mission to make decorating fun and easy." 

Farr's travels inspired the collection, which features elements such as exotic animals, forests, and Icelandic glaciers.
