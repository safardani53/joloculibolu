Lorraine Kelly gropes her own boobs in hilarious RuPaul's Drag Race UK appearance

 Posted on July 19, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  349 words  |  Larita Shotwell

LORRAINE Kelly had fans in hysterics with her hilarious appearance on RuPaul's Drag Race UK this evening.

The 59-year-old star showed off her fun side as she groped her own boobs during the programme's much-anticipated Snatch Game.

Snatch Game is an iconic part of each Drag Race series, and is a parody of US game show Match Game that sees contestants try to match fill-in-the-blank answers with the celebrity panellists.

On the show, the celebrities are actually the Drag Race hopefuls dressed up as their favourite stars - with tonight's game including impersonations of Gemma Collins, Sir David Attenborough, and Mary Berry.

Lorraine was a contestant in the game alongside Stacey Dooley, and looked sensational in a silver glitter dress with a plunging neckline.

Introducing the star, Ru asked Lorraine if she was scared, to which the breakfast TV presenter giggled: "I'm so excited!

"I'm not actually scared."

Ru jokingly hit back: "Well, you should be."

Later in the game, Lorraine was given the following sentence to fill in the blank: "Alan Carr is SO camp, yesterday I caught him putting one of my wigs on his BLANK!"

Keeping things innocent, Lorraine guessed: "I'd say puppies".

To which one of the queens quipped: "Lorraine, you have marvellous puppies."

In response, the star laughed as she groped her own chest and thanked the contestant.

The moment drove viewers wild, with one tweeting: "That was one of the funniest snatch games and drag race ever..! Lorraine Kelly’s puppies!!!"
