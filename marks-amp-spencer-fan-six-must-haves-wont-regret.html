 Posted on May 31, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  370 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

DECIDING what new products to give a go during your weekly food shop can be quite a risk.

But if you're an M&S fan then you may just be in a luck...because one shopper named Georgina Honeyman has revealed her six food and cleaning must-haves from the high-street store.

First up on her list, which was shared to TikTok, is the 'M&S Ready to Bake Belgian Milk Chocolate Cookie Dough.'

The irresistibly indulgent vanilla cookie dough is packed with Belgian milk chocolate pieces and cooks in 14-16 minutes, making a total of six cookies.

And what's more is it's gluten free and suitable for vegetarians - win win!

With this in mind, it's little surprise Georgina rated it a solid "11/10."

Next up, the M&S shopper notes how she can't pop into M&S without picking up a bag of 'sour cream & jalapeño combo mix.'

The mouth-watering crisp snacks come in different shapes and are seasoned with sour cream and jalapeños.

Georgina proceeds to point out that she's also a huge fan of the M&S 'sweet chilli houmous.'

The medium-spiced dip, which is suitable for vegans, is made with crushed chickpeas, sesame seed paste, sweet chilli sauce and jalapeño chilli peppers, and is recommended to be served with flatbread or pittas.

Not stopping there, Georgina also has high praise for the M&S 'garlic and chilli flavoured olive oil,' which is perfect for drizzling over pizza or pasta dishes.

Moving away from food, she then shares her top picks when it comes to laundry and cleaning products - including the '3 in 1 bio colour laundry capsules' and 'sensitive multi-surface cleaner.'

The video has since gone viral, racking up a whopping 57,000 views and several comments, with many keen to offer their opinions on the six products mentioned.

"The sour cream and jalapeño crisps sell out SO QUICK in my store but they’re so good!" wrote one.

Georgina replied: "Genuinely the best - so addictive."

A second quipped: "Why am I only seeing this now?"

A third penned: ""Yummmmm."

Meanwhile, a fourth commented: "Omg GF cookie dough."
