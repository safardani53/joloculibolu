BACHELOR In Paradise fans have been left in shock by one contestant's NSFW entrance.

Jacob Rapini, 28, ditched his clothes altogether as he introduced himself butt-naked on the season premiere.

Making one of the boldest entrances in Bachelor Nation history, the mortgage broker from Scottsdale, Arizona, ran down to the beach where numerous other contestants had already arrived.

Wearing nothing but a leaf covering his crotch, he beat his chest and yelled loudly and he sprinted down the sands.

The unexpected outfit caused Bachelor in Paradise producers to censor his bare butt and groin with a black box.

"Tarzan is here!" he announced.
