 Posted on May 1, 2024  |  20 minutes  |  4092 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

FROM Lisa Marie Presley, Tony Bennett, and Lance Reddick, to Tina Turner, Pee-Wee Herman, and Angus Cloud, 2023 has been filled with several tragic passings.

As the year enters its final days, we take a look at all of the television, film, and musical icons that we have lost.

Fred White

Fred White was a musician and songwriter best known as one of the early members of Earth, Wind & Fire.

He died on January 1, 2023, at the age of 67 from an unknown cause.

Lola Chantrelle Mitchell, better known by the stage name Gangsta Boo, was a rapper best known for her role in the hip-hop group Three 6 Mafia.

She passed away on January 1, 2023, at the age of 43 from an unknown cause.
