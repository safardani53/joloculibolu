Khloe Kardashian: Wrist tattoo

In KUWTK season 16 episode 1 Khloe, Kourtney and their friends head to Palm Springs.

Their momager, Kris, just bought a brand new holiday home in Calfornia and the girls were ready to party the night away in the luxury crib.

Screen Shot: KUWTK season 16 episode 1 – Hayu

While driving Kourtney and the rest of the girls to Palm Springs, Khloe chows down on some pizza and shows off a touching wrist tattoo.

Screen Shot: KUWTK season 16 episode 1 – Hayu

What does Khloe’s tattoo say?

If you look closely enough you’ll see that Khloe’s tatt reads “I love you” which could lead to people thinking that she had some ink done for her partner, Tristan Thompson.

After all, when Khloe was with Lamar Odom they did get matching couple’s tattoos on their hands.

Khloe had his initials “LO” etched onto her hand but has since had it removed.

Screen Shot: KUWTK season 16 episode 1 – HayuScreen Shot: KUWTK S16 E1 Khloe Kardashian tattoo

Khloe Kardashian’s wrist tattoo meaning

The real meaning behind Khloe’s wrist tattoo isn’t actually anything to do with her baby’s father, Tristan.

She got the ink done in memory of her late father, Robert Kardashian, in 2008 according to mammamia.com and the tattoo is written in Robert’s handwriting.

Robert sadly died in 2003 from oesophagal cancer.

Does Khloe have any other tattoos?

It doesn’t look as though Khloe has any more tattoos.

According to The Sun, when Khloe was 16 years old she did get the word “Daddy” tattooed on her lower back as well as a cross symbol with her friend, Nicole Ritchie.

Unsurprisingly, she later got the regretful ink laser-removed!
