What engagement ring did Travis Barker give Kourtney Kardashian?

 Posted on March 19, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  685 words  |  Aldo Pusey

KOURTNEY Kardashian and Travis Barker had their star-studded wedding on May 22, 2022, after tieing the knot in April in a Las Vegas chapel.

Kardashian's rock star beau popped the question in Montecito, California, on October 17, 2021.

What does Kourtney Kardashian's engagement ring look like?

Travis Barker's teenage daughter Alabama was the first to give fans a glimpse of the ring after posting a video to the tune of Bruno Mars' hit Marry You.

In the clip, which is captioned "so happy for you guys! I love u both!", you can see Kourtney rocking a massive oval-shaped diamond on her left hand.

The ring is reported to cost a whopping $925720.

Kourtney's sister Kim Kardashian later posted a video on Twitter zooming in on the ring, giving fans an even closer look.

What happened at Kourtney and Travis Barker's engagement party?

Travis, best known as the drummer in the rock band Blink-182, popped the question on a public beach in Montecito, California.

Fellow beachgoers spotted the pair surrounded by red roses and white candles as the rocker got down on one knee.

Later on, it looks like the couple enjoyed an intimate engagement dinner.

Travis' two kids - Alabama and Landon - shared snaps of the dinner party on their social media pages.

It looks like Atiana De La Hoya, Barker's 23-year-old former stepdaughter with whom he remains close, was also invited.

All four of Kourtney's famous sisters - Kim, Khloe, Kylie, and Kendall - were in attendance, as well as "momager" Kris Jenner.

What do the other Kardashian sisters' engagement rings look like?

Kim Kardashian

In November 2022, Kim divorced her third husband, rapper Kanye West. The reality mogul received two different engagement rings from her ex.

In 2013, when Kanye proposed, Kim received an impressive 15-carat oval-cut ring.

Two years after the pair tied the knot, Kim received a second ring - this time, it was a 20-carat cushion-cut diamond worth a staggering $4million.

However, Kim was left heartbroken after the second ring was stolen during a trip to Paris, France in a raid where she lost $10 million worth of jewelry.

Kim was never able to recover the rock but had a replica made later.

Before marrying Kanye, Kim was briefly married to basketball star Kris Humphries, who reportedly paid around $2million for a square-shaped ring from Lorraine Schwartz. for the star.

After Kim filed for divorce just 72 days into their marriage, the ring was resold at auction for less than half of its original price.

Her first husband Damon Thomas also bought her a diamond engagement ring.

Khloe Kardashian

Khloe was married to NBA star Lamar Odom for seven years from 2009 to 2016.

Khloe, who famously wed Lamar after a whirlwind one-month romance, was gifted a 12.5-carat radiant-cut ring worth a reported $850,000 from her ex.

The reality star sent tongues wagging in April 2021 after sporting what appeared to be another engagement ring - this time from on-off beau Tristan Thompson, with whom she shares a daughter, True Thompson, and a son via surrogate.

The two split for good in July 2021 after a series of cheating scandals rocked their relationship.

When did Kourtney and Travis get married?

In April 2022, Kourtney and Travis got married in a Las Vegas chapel with an Elvis impersonator to officiate the "practice" wedding.

TMZ reported Travis and Kourtney walked down the aisle on Monday, April, a day after being spotted together at the Grammy Awards.

The following month, the two legalized their wedding at a California courthouse.

On May 22, the two had a star-studded wedding in Portofino, Italy.

Kourtney was seen dressed in a gorgeous mini dress with a long veil designed by Dolce & Gabanna costing $1900.

Their celebration lasted a couple of days and the two had festivities planned for their close family and friends.

Hulu released a wedding special called Kourtney and Travis: Till Death Do Us Part which documents their extravagant event.
