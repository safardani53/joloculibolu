Tim McGraw’s Abs & Muscles On ‘Men’s Health’

Who knew working out with a kettle ball looked like that. Faith Hill‘s hubby, 47, stunned in the magazine shoot and revealed that it hasn’t been an easy journey to become one of the hottest men in country music (and all music, in my opinion). I mean, he actually called his nipples “diamond cutters!”

For Tim, he’d drink to loosen himself up on stage, which of course didn’t help him get in shape — but luckily his family pulled him together!

“When your wife tells you it’s gone too far, that’s a big wake-up call,” he said about Faith’s advice to help him shape up. “That, and realizing you’re gonna lose everything you have. Not monetarily, not career-wise, but family-wise. It got to the point where my kids were getting older, and it was way past the point that they noticed it. And I noticed that they noticed.”

The “they” he’s referring to is his adorable family — Faith, and their three daughters Gracie, 17, Maggie, 15, and Audrey, 12.

Tim’s Mom Warned Him About Drinking & Drugs

“I remember my mom tellin’ me, ‘Y’know, if you don’t get up there a couple of times without doing that, it’s gonna be a problem,’ ” he told the mag, adding that it pretty much did become a problem. “I drank too much. I partied too much. And did other things too much. Chemically. No needles or that kind of stuff, but . . . use your imagination.”

It’s pretty clear he doesn’t anymore, because with that body, he’s definitely being healthy and working hard. Again, at 47, it’s no surprise that he showed Faith how he may be a real bad boy and a real good man.
