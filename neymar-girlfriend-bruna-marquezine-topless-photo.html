Neymar's stunning girlfriend Bruna Marquezine cheers PSG star up after Real Madrid loss with topless

 Posted on September 26, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  344 words  |  Martina Birk

BRUNA MARQUEZINE cheered up her Paris Saint-Germain star boyfriend Neymar by uploading a TOPLESS photo on Instagram.

The 26-year-old forward was forced to watch his side crash out of the Champions League at the hands of Real Madrid from the sidelines on Tuesday evening.

Neymar is currently recuperating after undergoing foot surgery, and was powerless as Zinedine Zidane's side ran out 2-1 winners at the Parc des Princes.

But his mood soon turned for the better, as Marquezine posted a snap of her standing topless, with her arms covering her modesty.

Captioning the photo, the model and actress said: "BREATHE."

While her partner replied on the picture, by commenting: "That photo," followed up with a loved-up emoji.

Marquezine's post was liked by more than 1.4million people, a small fraction of her 26.7million followers.

The image was part of a series of topless photos of the model taken by her photographer friend Bruna Moreira.
