TEEN Mom Jenelle Evans has taken to social media to post a sad selfie following a brutal fight with her husband, David Eason.

In Jenelle's latest Instagram story she appeared to look heartbroken with eyes of heavy sadness despite it being her 31st birthday over-the-weekend.

For the photo the star had on what appeared to be a flannel and her hair somewhat messily fell around her shoulders.

The former MTV notable seemed to be in a solemn mood although she posted about what was supposed to be her special day.

Even though the mother-of-three appeared to be down in the snap she still shared her gratitude to fans who honored her on her 31st birthday.

"Thanks for all the birthday wishes [heart emoji]," Jenelle wrote in her Instagram story.
