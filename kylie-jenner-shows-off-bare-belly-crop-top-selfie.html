KYLIE Jenner has flaunted her bare belly in a crop top and low-rise jeans for a new selfie after fans claimed she annouced her pregnancy.

The makeup mogul took to her Instagram Stories on Tuesday to show off her afternoon outfit.

In the mirror selfie, Kylie, 26, showed off her fit figure as she leaned to the side and posed seductively for the photo.

The Kardashians star flaunted her chiseled stomach in a tight white tank top and went for a more relaxed look with a pair of loose-fitting blue jeans.

She allowed her wavy, dark brunette hair to frame her moderately made-up face as she held one side back with her hand and let the other fall naturally over her cheek.

Kylie appeared ready to head out of her home as she also sported a bejeweled black handbag.

The appearance of Kylie's flat tummy seemed to prove that the TV personality isn't pregnant, something that fans have speculated in recent weeks.

Last week, the Hulu star managed to trick fans into thinking she was announcing a new pregnancy on social media after she took to Instagram with a repost promoting her new clothing line, Khy.

Kylie initially launched the line on November 1, adding two new drops since.

The photo showed Kylie's friend, Lauren Perez, posing in a matching red zip-up sweatshirt and sweatpants with a white T-shirt rolled up underneath.

The Kardashian pal had her belly bump uncovered and on full display, as she snapped the mirror selfie.

Lauren tagged both Kylie and her clothing brand in the shot, showing she was wearing items from the brand-new line.

While the reshared photo was clearly not of the Kylie Cosmetics founder, many followers thought it was her at first glance and took it as a pregnancy announcement.

Taking to Reddit, one person wrote: "When I first saw this on Kylie’s Story I thought it was Kylie revealing a baby bump and promoting Khy. I had to do a double take."

Others chimed in, with one person even thinking the photo was of Kylie's sister Kendall Jenner, 28.

PREGNANCY SPECULATION

This chatter comes as many fans believe Kylie has been hiding a possible pregnancy over the last few weeks.

The Khy founder, who is currently dating actor Timothee Chalamet, 27, shares her daughter Stormi, five, and son Aire, one, with her ex Travis Scott, 32.

The former couple called it quits in December 2022 but have been co-parenting their kids.

Kylie has had several rumors regarding a supposed pregnancy circulating since her relationship with Timothee went public earlier this year.

Fans first discussed pregnancy rumors after the star made a public appearance.

The business mogul recently accepted her Wall Street Journal Magazine Innovator Award, and fans were quick to spot a detail suggesting she might be expecting.

Kylie attended the event with her boyfriend and posted some shots of the night on Instagram.

In the photos, the star wore her hair down along with a bold, plum lip, and a dark brown dress from her new brand Khy.

She captioned the post: "What a night. thank you @wsjmag for the award and honor tonight of being this year's brand innovator and for recognizing @khy."

When the photos were posted on a Reddit forum dedicated to the Kardashians, fans were quick to notice that Kylie held her hand over her stomach during the evening.

One fan wrote: "Kylie cradling her stomach- lil Timothee?"

SKINNY KY

Kylie's oversized sweatpants nearly fell off her thinning frame in photos for her Khy clothing line.

In an Instagram post captioned, "KHY DROP 003!!!! available NOW on khy.com @khy," Kylie modeled the recently released apparel offered by her new clothing brand.

The reality TV star flaunted her six-pack abs and pencil-thin waist while showing off the casual and comfy outfits.

Oversized red sweatpants hung from Kylie's waits in one photo while a baggy white hoodie nearly swallowed up her petite frame in another.

She was shown wearing several different styles of crop tops in the snapshots.

One was longsleeved, another loose-fitting, and a third, skin-tight option was shown in black and white.

All three managed to accentuate the Keeping Up With the Kardashians alum's fit figure.

The beauty mogul also revealed her thin legs while wearing Khy's fleece shorts, an item she would later pair with the brand's ribbed tank and baby tee.
