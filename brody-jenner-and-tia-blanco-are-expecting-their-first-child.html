Brody Jenner And Tia Blanco Are Expecting Their First Child

 Posted on April 25, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  458 words  |  Martina Birk

Brody Jenner, an American media star, is expecting his first child with his fiancée, Tia Blanco. On January 1, the 39-year-old actor rushed to Instagram to post a video of Blanco receiving a scan of her womb. Brody wished his followers a happy new year in the post, while also confirming a future addition to his family.

“We are thrilled to share the gift of a new life with you in the new year… Our little angel has arrived. New Year’s greetings!”

Several friends and family members commented on Brody Jenner’s post in the comments area. Linda Thompson, Jenner’s mother, commented:

“I’m so glad you’re spreading the word so I can inform everyone I see on the street! Lol I’ve been trying to keep this a secret, but it’s been difficult! We are overjoyed! I adore you both… I adore all three of you!”

Madison, a musician, stated:

“Best wishes!!!!! Baby already has the greatest genes and skills.”

Tracy Mills stated:

“What a gift @brodyjenner, you two will be wonderful parents. Continue to shine.”

Brody Jenner works as a DJ.

Brody Jenner is a well-known television personality who has been on series such as The Hills, Keeping Up with the Kardashians, Bromance, and Princes of Malibu. Fans have long wondered what the 39-year-old did for a profession or whether he was surviving off the money handed to him by his famous father, Caitlyn Jenner. Brody is a “highly sought-after” DJ, according to his Hills: New Beginnings profile (via Refinery 29). Jenner started his DJ career in 2014, when he began playing at various places alongside his buddy DJ William Lifestyle. Haute Living claimed at the time that Jenner’s love of music drove him to a career as a DJ. He’d also played in Cabo San Lucas and Los Angeles, and he’d started publicizing his concerts on Instagram.

According to numerous news sites, Brody Jenner received DJ residencies in Las Vegas in 2015 at prominent clubs such as Tao Nightclub and The Bank Nightclub. Since then, he has been DJing all across the nation, with the most regular appearances in Atlantic City and Las Vegas. In addition, Brody Jenner joined the musical group AHZ with his buddy Adam O’Rourke in 2018. Loren Moore and DJ Savi have joined the pair to form a new outfit. His net worth is estimated to be $10 million by Celebrity Net Worth.

Brody and Tia Blanco initially started relationship rumors in April 2022, according to his personal life. In May, the couple made their relationship public on Instagram by posting a photo of themselves standing with Jenner’s dog. Before dating Tia Blanco, the TV celebrity was married to Kaitlynn Carter for a year, from 2018 to 2019. Carter and her partner, Kristopher Brock, are expecting their second child.
