Born on January 26, 1961, Wayne Gretzky became a Canadian former professional ice hockey player and retired head coach. He played the entire 20 seasons in the National Hockey League (NHL) and four teams between 1979 and 1999.

Moreover, he is regarded as one of the greatest and influential hockey players by other players, sportswriters, and the NHL itself. He has also been the leading scorer in the history of NHL with maximum goals. His contribution to the sport is maximum, but in 1999, when he was retired, he held 61 NHL records.
