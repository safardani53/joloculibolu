Bratz Pride x JimmyPaul: When does the collection release and how can I buy it?

 Posted on July 23, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  439 words  |  Larita Shotwell

JUNE is Pride Month in the United States, and MGA Entertainment announced that Bratz is releasing a Pride collection.

The Bratz Pride x JimmyPaul collection is a historical collection as it will feature the first fashion doll same-sex couple.

When is the collection released and how can I buy it?

This historical collection will be available beginning June 1, 2022, on the first official day of Pride Month.

Bratz Pride x JimmyPaul dolls will be available exclusively at online retailers globally.

Customers can visit the Bratz official website to shop for the dolls.

Bratz dolls Roxxi and Nevra made their debut as the first Bratz girlfriends in 2020, and now the dolls are coming in physical form.

Roxxi wears knee-high socks in the collection that feature the official lesbian pride colors of pink, orange, white, and red.

She also wears a fuzzy floor-length scarf in traditional rainbow colors.

Roxxi holds a sign that says: "Ugh... I can't even think straight."

Roxxi's girlfriend Nevra is seen wearing a long coat in rainbow colors.

Nevra's sign reads: "Queer & unapologetic."

This doll also wears a beach hat with rainbow colors that include a nonbinary flag pin, intersex, and gender-fluid buttons.

Both dolls are holding hands and are said to be girlfriends per the press release.

The Pride collection includes the dolls, two dolls, stands, an official Bratz certificate, and Jimmy Paul illustrated packaging.

Their "rally-ready accessories" include a megaphone, fan, water, champagne, and posters.

Who is Jimmy Paul?

Jimmy Paul is a designer from Amsterdam.

Paul has worked with many high fashion magazines including Vogue, Elle, and Paper Magazine.

The designer is responsible for the illustrations and the clothing designs of the Bratz Pride collection.

In order to support the LGBT community, MGA Entertainment's charity MGA Cares is donating $25,000 to The Trevor Project.

The Trevor Project is the world's largest suicide prevention and mental health organization for LGBT youth.

When were Bratz dolls first released?

Bratz first launched the original dolls on May 21, 2001.

The original four dolls include Yasmin, Cloe, Jade, and Sasha.

On May 21, 2022, the Bratz dolls celebrated their 21st birthday.

Bratz dolls are known for their almond-shaped eyes, eyeshadow, and big glossy lips.
