Where to buy Yeezy 350 dazzling blue sneakers

 Posted on May 28, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  421 words  |  Larita Shotwell

KANYE West’s latest Yeezys’s are set to go on sale later this week.

But where can you get them and how much do they cost? Here’s what we know.

When do Yeezy 350 Dazzling Blue sneakers go on sale?

The Yeezy 350 dazzling blue sneakers will go on sale on Saturday, February 26, 2022, on Adidas.com.

It comes six years after Kanye and the German sportswear giant's released the Adidas Yeezy Boost 350 back in 2016.

Pictures show that the latest Yeezy has a black knitted upper to it with a blue stripe on the side.

Shoe retailers Sole say: "A black snug adidas Primeknit upper serves as the base, while the signature “SPLY-350” stripe branding across the side panels take on a royal blue hue.

“Elsewhere, the laces and lining blend in the background which makes the icy accent pop even more.

“A full-length comfortable Boost cushioning housed in a translucent black midsole completes the pair.”

Where to buy Yeezy 350 Dazzling Blue sneakers?

Fans can sign up to buy the sneakers now via Adidas’ CONFIRMED app.

They can also purchase the sneakers from Adidas’ official website upon release.

The shoe will be available in all family sizes – adults, kids and infants.

The price of the Yeezy 350 V2 for adults is $230, $160 for kids and $140 for infants.

Fans not willing to stump the cash, can also opt for an online raffle through websites like DTLR, Bodega, Footpatrol and Sevenstore.

This is because buying them in person from retails stores is difficult due to high demand.

What other Yeezy 350 sneakers are there?

The sought-after sneakers and clothing pieces have become incredibly famous and are often sported by celebrities, including everyone from the Kardashians to Justin Bieber.

As a result, the sneakers come in a vast array of colours and styles.

Some of the sneakers which have already been released include the Yeezy Boost 700 MNVN Black, Yeezy 700 V3 Dark Glow, and Yeezy 500 Soft Vision.

The Yeezy Boost 350 V2 was available in the colourways Tail Light, Zebra, Synth RF, and Glow - and almost all of them sold out immediately.
