 Posted on April 25, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  463 words  |  Aldo Pusey

COMEDIAN Pete Holmes has stopped Sunday's Celebrity Family Feud episode to feud against Steve Harvey.

He accused Steve of accepting an answer that didn't fit the prompt in a hot-headed attack.

On August 13, comedian Pete and his family competed against actors Jared and Genevieve Padalecki.

Midway through the Celebrity Family Feud episode, Pete of HBO's Crashing fame came crashing down over a survey's results.

Jared - of Gilmore Girls, Supernatural, and Walker popularity - and his team were asked: "Tell me a scent you'd be shocked to see as an air freshener."

One Padelecki team member - Michelle - guessed: “Microwaved fish?" surprising host Steve, 66.

“Food/broccoli” appeared on the board as the number one answer, giving the Padeleckis 29 points.

'SO, ANY FOOD GETS THE ANSWER?'

Pete then stopped the game show in its tracks and yelled from the other side of the stage: “So any food? "Any food gets the answer?

"That is nonsense.

"I say butter, cream, food? Fish?

“Steve, I feel you with me on this."

Pete began making guttural noises implying Family Feud would have accepted anything at all.

"Why not any sound gets the number one answer!? Just go 'Argh argh argh' number one answer," he continued.

"I'm so sorry," Steve helplessly told the audience and staff.

Opponent Jared then joked: “Number four is 'argh argh argh,'” making a bizarre noise of his own.

The hilarious throwaway guess from Jared diffused the tension and made Steve lose it, buckling over and laughing.

Steve then made a guttural noise, but it wasn't on the board, concluding the biting breakdown.
