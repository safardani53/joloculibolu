Celebrate St. Patrick Day with these hilarious memes that you can send to your friends and family on March 17. Keep reading for more.

St. Patrick's Day started out as a Christian holiday to celebrate the life of St. Patrick and the spreading of Christianity to Ireland. Today, it has evolved into a celebration of all things Irish, including leprechauns, shamrocks, the color green, and St. Patrick himself.

The mainstay of every year's St. Patrick's Day celebration includes wearing green and going on a bar crawl to imbibe as many green beers as humanly possible.
