Kim Dickens’ Biography: Where is Kim Dickens from?

Born as Kimberly Jan Dickens on June 18, 1965, in Huntsville, Alabama, Kim Dickens is the daughter of father Justin Dickens and mother Pam (Clark) Howell. Regarding her nationality, she is an American nationality and belongs to the white ethnicity.

She attended Lee High School and following her graduation from high school, she went to the Vanderbilt University in Nashville, Tennessee. She graduated from the university earning a bachelor of arts in communications.

After graduation, she moved to New York City, where she studied at the Lee Strasberg Theatre and Film Institute. She worked as a waitress to continue her studies and completed her graduation from the American Academy of Dramatic Arts.
