Khloe Kardashian Cuts Off Lamar’s Credit Card Access

With Lamar Odom‘s drug addiction, it’s been taking a toll on the couple’s bank account — so Khloe Kardashian had to take a stand and do something about it to try and make a point.

“Khloe put a hold on all their credit cards so he couldn’t get cash,” says a mutual friend to UsWeekly. Since his account got frozen, he’s moved on to pawning Khloe’s jewelry to buy drugs and pay down debt to his dealers.

Since Lamar, 33, has left their Tarzana, Calif. mansion they shared, she hasn’t seen him since he was arrested for a DUI, so cutting him off is the only way she can somewhat communicate with him and get her emotions across.

Lamar Pawns Jewelry To Get Cash For Drugs

Because Khloe froze him out of their joint account, he got extremely angry, a source tells Star. “Lamar became infuriated and stole some of her jewelry in a fit of rage to sell for drugs. He’s spent $1,000 a day at times, and he still owes thousands to his dealers.” How terrible.

By pawning jewelry for drugs, that will make Khloe completely distrust Lamar and make it that much harder for their marriage to get back on track. We hope that Lamar gets the help he needs soon so his relationship with Khloe can try to be saved.
