Where can I buy tickets?

On February 4, 2022, Faker announced that the Hotel Surrender tour had been postponed for the fall due to the Covid pandemic.

The tour is now set to start on September 8, 2022.

Fans can purchase tickets directly through TicketMaster or visit the artist's website.

Nicholas James Murphy, who professionally goes by Chet Faker, wrote about the tour on his Instagram in February.
