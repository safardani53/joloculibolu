BRANDI Glanville has posted a new video that is making fans do a double take. Her face has completely transformed.

Brandi released a teaser trailer for a new episode of her podcast, Brandi Glanville Unfiltered, on her Instagram.

Her special guest was Farrah Brittany Aldjufrie, daughter of Kyle Richards who is also on RHOBH. Farrah can also be seen in Netflix's Buying Beverly Hills.

On the podcast, the pair discuss RHOBH, the Netflix show, and what it's like to work with family. Farrah also gives Brandi an update on her wedding plans.

Further on in the conversation, they talk about a Real Housewives revamp with a new generation starring Farrah and her cousin Paris Hilton.

Brandi begins the clip by saying: "You're a legit professional. Unlike myself."
